## 💼 LINKEDIN — Launch Post

**Post:**

We just launched Mr.Tour Guide — and it started with one question:

**What about the people travel forgot?**

People with disabilities. The elderly. Students who can't afford a ticket. For them, travel content is a window they can look through but never open.

So we rebuilt what a travel video is:

📳 **Real-feel haptics** — an ML pipeline processes every creator upload and generates a haptic track from its audio and motion. Waves feel like waves. Your phone becomes the instrument.

🥽 **MR/VR mode** — place the Taj Mahal in your living room through your camera, or go fully immersive on a headset.

♿ **Accessibility as a hard constraint** — feel-intensity, sound, reduce-motion and dark-theme settings that *always* override creator defaults. Live weather for every destination. Minimal AI overviews instead of walls of links.

🎬 **A real creator economy** — one login, two roles. Creators get a web studio + in-app tools: upload video/VR/MR captures, set city covers, tune how each video feels, and preview exactly what travelers will experience. Role-gated communities for both sides.

Under the hood: Flutter, a Dart API compiled to a single binary, Neon Postgres, Cloudflare R2, Groq-powered AI search, nginx + Cloudflare edge — with DPDP Act 2023 consent, email verification and rate limiting from day one.

This is v1. The haptic tracks ship today as the contract for the ML models we're training next — and wearable support (haptic vests, gloves) is on the roadmap.
